About

Hi there, my name is Gianna, but you can call me Gi! I am passionately committed to exploring how art plays an instrumental role in who we become. This led me to pursue an interdisciplinary Master’s degree in art history, religion and philosophy at King’s College London, taught in collaboration with The National Gallery. I currently find myself in the Sotheby’s Valuations Department, whilst writing tours and performing research for The Ashmolean Museum, The Estorick Collection of Modern Italian Art, and The Victoria & Albert Museum.

My research interests include 20th century Italian sculpture and drawings, spirituality and mysticism, sacred and devotional images, the history of belief, and the role that works of art play in religious culture.
